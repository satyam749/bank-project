Early Life and Education:

Elon Musk was born in Pretoria, South Africa, in 1971. He developed an interest in computers at a young age, creating a video game at age 12 and selling it to a computer magazine. He later moved to Canada and then the United States for higher education, graduating from the University of Pennsylvania with a Bachelor of Arts and a Bachelor of Science degree.

SpaceX:

Musk is the founder, CEO, and chief engineer of SpaceX, a space exploration company. The website's banner showcases SpaceX's rocket launches, space missions, and future space travel plans.

Tesla:

As the co-founder and CEO of Tesla, Musk oversees the design and manufacturing of electric vehicles and battery products. The company's website features a banner highlighting their latest products and initiatives.

X Corp. (formerly Twitter):

X Corp. is the parent company of the social media platform X.com, which Musk purchased and rebranded. The website's banner and overall design reflect the "X" branding.
